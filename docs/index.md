# Incurred Cost Submissions Training

Welcome to ICS Training — the third site in a series of government contracting training tools.

**Start at the [Dashboard](/dashboard)** to choose your level and begin learning.

## The curriculum arc

| Site | Topic |
|------|-------|
| [FAR 31 Training](https://far31-training.vercel.app) | Cost classification — allowable, direct, indirect, unallowable |
| [Pool Rates Training](https://pool-rates-training.vercel.app) | Building and applying indirect cost rates |
| **ICS Training** (this site) | Reporting actual costs to the government at year-end |

## Three levels. Eight modules each.

- **Beginner** — What ICS is, who files, and how it works
- **Intermediate** — Schedule mechanics, rate variances, and deficiency avoidance
- **Advanced** — Multi-segment filings, DCAA audits, and M&A scenarios
